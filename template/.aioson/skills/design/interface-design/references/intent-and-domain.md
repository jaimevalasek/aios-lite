# Intent and Domain

Before touching layout or tokens, answer three questions with specificity:

1. Who is this human?
2. What must they accomplish?
3. What should this feel like?

Then define:
- 5+ domain concepts
- 5+ colors that naturally belong to the domain
- 1 signature element
- 3 defaults to avoid

If the product cannot be identified after removing its name, the visual direction is still generic.
