# Design Directions

Pick one direction and commit.
Do not mix multiple directions in the same screen.

## Precision and Density
For dashboards, admin panels, and power-user tools.

## Warmth and Approachability
For consumer products, onboarding, and collaborative tools.

## Sophistication and Trust
For fintech, enterprise SaaS, and products that need calm authority.

## Premium Dark Platform
For premium operational products where contrast and control matter more than novelty.

The chosen direction must define spacing, typography, border strategy, depth, and motion posture before components are built.
