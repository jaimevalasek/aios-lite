02 / Organization

One home for every file, doc, and decision.

Files, specs, briefs, and links — structured the way your project actually works. ORBIT's document model is flexible enough for any team, with enough structure to keep things findable six months later.

03 / Communication

Decisions happen in context — not in threads.

Comments, approvals, and feedback attach directly to the work they're about. No more hunting through Slack for the decision made two weeks ago. The project is the record of truth.
