# Research: Harness Engineering 2026

## Verdict
✓ Decisions validated against recent research. The "Harness-First" approach is the industry standard in April 2026.

## Findings
- **Nautilus Pattern:** Separation of agents into Planner, Executor, Verifier (Critic), and Governor.
- **Closed-Loop Systems:** Shift from instructions (what) to sensors (how it stays on track) using real-world feedback.
- **Entropy Management:** Need for periodic "Garbage Collection" to prevent architectural drift.
- **Steering Loops:** Engineer's role moves from fixing code to "steering the harness" by refining tests/linters.
- **A2A Reviews:** Agent-to-Agent peer review is critical for large-scale autonomous builds.
